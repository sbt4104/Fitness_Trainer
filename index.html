Understanding Your Results
Body Mass Index (BMI)
Measures body fat based on height and weight. Normal: 18.5-24.9, Overweight: 25-29.9, Obese: 30+
Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR)
Calories your body burns at rest. Used to calculate daily caloric needs and weight management goals.
Total Daily Calories
Total calories needed including activity. Eat below this for weight loss, above for weight gain.
Skeletal Muscle Mass
Percentage of body weight that's muscle. Healthy ranges: Men 45-55%, Women 40-50%.
Daily Protein Target
Daily protein needed to maintain/build muscle. Based on skeletal muscle mass × 1.5g per pound.
Heart Rate Training Zones
Zone 1-2: Recovery/Endurance, Zone 3: Aerobic, Zone 4: Threshold, Zone 5: Power/Sprint training.
BMI Weight Ranges
Healthy: BMI 23-25, Athletic: BMI 25-29. Use "Calculate Ideal Weight" for personalized ranges.
